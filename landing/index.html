How it works

From selection to insight
in three seconds

LensAI is designed for zero friction. No copying, no pasting, no switching tabs. Just select and understand.

1

Draw a box

Press the shortcut to activate LensAI, then drag to select any region on your screen. Works on any content — code, images, charts, text, anything visible in Chrome.

Pick from ELI5, Technical, Summary, Code Review, or Translate. LensAI auto-detects the content type and suggests the best mode — you can always switch after.
